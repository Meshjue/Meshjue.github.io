     Hi, my name is Matthew McCallaCreary and I am an aspiring Digital Media Art student at San Jose State University. I have been enamored by art mediums such as video games, film, music, photography, and illustrations for as long as I’ve been alive. 
     I have been engaged in art classes since preschool, but once I got to highschool, I began to focus more attention on developing my craft. At the time, I did not have a very good idea of what my options were as an artist, so I took my art teacher's advice and chose to focus on Graphic Design. To cultivate my skills, I attended the California College of Arts: Pre College Graphic Design Program, and an AP2D class at my highschool. It was not until after these courses that I felt I was on the wrong path. It was like something snapped in my soul, and it made me disregard my love for art completely. I never had a very high self esteem to begin with, and I had just lost the only thing I ever thought I was good at. I did not feel like I could turn to my friends or family, because nobody seemed to care about what I did. It was since then, my junior year in highschool, that I had felt devastated, alone, and lost. I hated myself. Above all else I hated art, because it reminded me of myself. A flaw. A waste of time. A mistake that never deserved so much attention. 
     I have since then rekindled my love and joy for most facets of art. Although, it is much less pure and holds more skepticism, it is strong. Through my own trials and tribulations inflicted by life and other personal demons, I saw that I had no other choice than to pursue art. I’d rather not list specifics, but in brief James Baldwin esk fashion, I realized I had no other role in life than to do so. As much as I wanted to run away from it, I could not ignore the creative hunger inside of me. 
     I will be the first to tell you that I do not think I am as skilled in select mediums as most of my other classmates. However, I now know that I am talented, extremely driven,  have the aptitude to learn, and am ready to grow as an artist. I want to join the Digital Media Arts program because I feel this major has the best tools and curriculum for me to properly express myself with conviction and grow as a person.
